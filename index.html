Sounds Like

"Sounds Like" was our third assignment in GBDA201. I chose to create an audio book episode capturing one of my grandfathers stories from his youth. I learned how quality audio is produced and layered. I included a reading, sound effects, and background ambiance to transport listeners to Emerson Manitoba.

During the editing phase of this project I learned a lot about Adobe Premiere Pro and its functions. Matching, stabilizing and remixing audio recordings is possible with the help of this application and made a big difference in the quality of my work.
